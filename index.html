Netlify Setup Instructions

To enable email confirmations, follow these steps in your Netlify dashboard:

  1. Go to your site's Forms section in Netlify dashboard
  2. Click on Form notifications
  3. Add a new Email notification
  4. Set the email template with variables like {{name}}, {{email}}, {{customerType}}
  5. Enable Outgoing notifications to send confirmations to users
  6. Optionally, set up Slack/Discord webhooks for instant notifications
